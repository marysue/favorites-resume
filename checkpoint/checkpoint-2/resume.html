Profile

Having a broad background in software engineering, starting as a Unix Systems Administrator, moving into Software Development has allowed me to quickly assimilate into complex environments quickly.

Having graduated from San Francisco State University, I spent 4 years programming in c and C++ in the gnu-unix and Visual Studio environments in financial systems and medical device enterprise development organizations.

After taking a 12 year sabatical to raise my family, I returned to software engineering. First, working customer support and technical assistance at an Internet Service Provider installing security certificates and websites in Microsoft IIS/Apache-Tomcat environments.

Recently, I worked for an educational services provider, providing customized citrix virtual environments, installing gateways, routers, domains, and desktops in both the Linux and Microsoft O/S's.

The final two years I moved into full-time development on an Agile development team working with Object Oriented programming. I developed software within a pre-designed framework, expanding and maintaining code in Java. Although having such versatility on the job was interesting, focusing on full-stack engineering was where I thrived. It was the perfect combination of using my past experience working with SQL, and c-based languages, but incorporated the front-end technologies HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JSP, JUnit, and XML.
